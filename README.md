# Amazon Sales Performance & Intelligence Dashboard



## 📌 Project Overview
Stepped into the role of a Business Analyst to transform a raw dump of 100 retail sales transactions across multiple regions, products, and customer profiles into a dynamic, automated business intelligence report. 

This project demonstrates data cleaning, multi-sheet relational lookups, advanced pivot reporting, and executive KPI design to optimize retail fulfillment and maximize effective sales.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Tech Stack
* **Advanced Excel:** Data validation, date grouping, custom metrics.
* **Formulas:** `XLOOKUP`, `IFERROR`, `AVERAGE`, `SUM`.
* **Business Intelligence:** Pivot Tables, Slicers, Conditional Formatting, and KPI Cards.

---

## 📊 Key Insights & Business Impact
* **The "North" Region Cancellation Crisis:** Identified that the North region has a critical **40% order cancellation rate**. While it remains the highest potential revenue generator, fixing logistics here yields the highest financial recovery.
* **Fulfillment Bottlenecks:** Found that **67.6% of deliveries are classified as "Slow"** (taking more than 2 days). 
* **Product Success:** **Electronics** is the dominant product category, generating **$15,209** in revenue, driven heavily by Smartphone and Smartwatch sales.

---

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations
1. **Address North Region Logistics:** Investigate last-mile delivery delays and allocate premium fulfillment partners to high-value items (like Smartphones) to reduce the 40% cancellation rate.
2. **Inventory Localization:** Warehouse high-demand categories (Electronics and Home) closer to high-volume regions (North and West) to increase the "Fast Delivery" rate above the current 32.35%.
3. **Courier Optimization:** Shift volume away from slower partners to optimize fulfillment velocity.
